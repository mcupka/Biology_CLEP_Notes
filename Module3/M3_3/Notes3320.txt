3.3.2 Societies

Society: an organization of individuals in a population. Tasks divided so group works together.

Insect Societies: bees, ants, wasps
-only queen breeds
-workers are her daughters
-different jobs, depending on age
	-nursery
	-cleaner
	-queen care
	-guard
	-forager

Primate Societies: built around concept of dominance
-more dominant individuals get best access to mates, resources
-individuals compete for status, often when sexual maturity reached by young
-altruistic behavior common when members related
