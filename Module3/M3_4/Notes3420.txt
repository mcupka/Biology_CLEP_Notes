3.4.2 Human Intervention in the Natural World

Human population size has profound effects on the environment
-pollution
-habitat loss
-overharvesting
-introduced species
-climate change

Pollution: addition of foreign substances to air, water, soil, etc
-started during industrial revolution
-fertilizers, pesticides, herbicides
-industrial cleaners
-carbon emmisions
-trash

We've had success repairing damaged ecosystems through the process of resource management
-learned ways to protect threatened species and ecosystems
-reduce, reuse, recycle
