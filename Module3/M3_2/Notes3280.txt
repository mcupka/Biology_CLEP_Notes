3.2.8 Evolutionary History of Humans

Order: Primates
Family: Great Apes (hominids)
Genus: Homo
Species: Sapiens

Oldest human fossils from Africa
-Suggest we evolved out of Africa 100,000 years ago, migrated throughout Europe, Asia, the Americas
-Crossed Bering Land Bridge into North America, then to Central & South America
