3.1.8 Evolutionary Ecology

Study of evolution of interactions between and among species and with their environment. How the interactions formed between species.
-competators
-mutualists
-predators
-prey
-pathogens

Two types studied:
1. Interactions with the physical environment
2. Interactions with other species
	-one-way
	-reciprocal

One-way interactions: one species affects another, but not vice versa
-Commensalism

Reciprocal interactions: both species are affected by the interaction
-predator-prey
-parasite-host
-competitors
